ACTIVITIES

Visit The Beach!

Of course, while you're act a beach town you defintely have to visit the beach! Onset beach is great for just about any beach activities you can think of. Swimming, building sand castles, playing volleyball, and much much more! And, once the sun begins to set, the beach can be a great place for a nice evening stroll.

Go Kayaking!

If you want to take your visit to Onset Beach to the next level, then you'll defeinetly want to consider renting out some kayaks. Onset Beach is an especially great place for those with an adventureous spirit to kayak, as you're able to visit and explore some of the small islands in the area. If this sounds like a fun time to you, then try checking out Nemasket Kayak Center in Onset!

Want to Watch Whales?

Tired of sitting around on the Shore? Try heading down to Plymoth for the day for some Whale Watching with Captain Johns Boats! This is a great way to get out on the open blue and have an experience you can't get anywhere else! Captain Johns Boats also offers other experiences on the water, such as Deep Sea Fishing and tours on a Paddlewheel Boat.
